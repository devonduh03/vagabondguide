AirBNB

It may not be easy, clean or cheap at times - but you can find single bedrooms for yourself ranging from $25 - $100 or more, but you get your own room and bed possibly with private bathroom. Another option is to share a room with multiple people; often that ranges from $15-$40 per night, not the cleanest or safest situations but its better then sleeping outside. airbnb.com

Tripalink

Tripalink offers furnished apartments and co-living spaces at relatively affordable prices still somewhat overpriced for what they offer but its a step above airbnb and motels.
